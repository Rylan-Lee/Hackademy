About Hackademy

Hackademy is a student-led tutoring program offering free coding and STEM education to young learners.

Founded by high school students, our goal is to spark curiosity, build problem-solving skills, and make tech education more accessible for everyone, no prior experience needed.

We focus on hands-on, beginner-friendly classes where students learn by building real projects. We offer both online and in-person options to meet students where they are.

Our tutors are passionate, trained volunteers dedicated to helping others grow. Hackademy also collaborates with schools, libraries, and local organizations to expand its reach.
